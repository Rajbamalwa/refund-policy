Nio Sports Refund Policy

At Nio Sports, we value our users and aim to provide a transparent and fair refund process for turf and ground bookings as well as bidding activities. Please read the policy carefully before making any bookings or bids.

1. Refund Scenarios

A. Booking canceled by the venue owner

  • If the booking is canceled by the venue owner, the user will receive a 100% refund including service fees.
  • The refund amount will be credited only to the user’s Nio Sports wallet within 48 hours.

B. User cancels the booking

  • Before 24 hours of the booking time:
    • Refund will be processed for the booking amount.
    • Service fees are non-refundable in this case.
    • Refund will be credited to the user’s wallet within 48 hours.
  • Within 24 hours of the booking time:
    • No cancellation or refund will be allowed.

C. Bidding-related refunds

  • If the first user books a slot and a second user places a bid:
    • Once the second user successfully wins the bid, the first user will get a 100% refund including service fees.
    • Refund will be credited to the first user’s wallet within 48 hours.
  • If the user wins a bid but later cancels:
    • Users can only win a bid within 3 hours of the booking time, so cancellations after winning are not allowed.
    • If cancellation happens before 24 hours (if applicable), the refund will be processed excluding service fees.

D. Non-refundable scenarios

  • Wrong booking details entered by the user: Refund allowed only if canceled before 24 hours (service fees non-refundable). No refund if canceled within 24 hours.
  • No-show at the venue: No refunds will be provided if the user fails to attend the booked slot.

2. Refund Timeline

All approved refunds will be processed within 48 hours and credited only to the user’s Nio Sports wallet.

3. Important Notes

  • Refunds will not be credited back to original payment methods such as bank accounts or UPI.
  • Service fees are refundable only if the booking is canceled by the venue owner.
  • Nio Sports reserves the right to amend or update this policy without prior notice.
